**A simple terminal based game (inspired from BINGO) played against a BOT.**

***HOW TO PLAY?***

***-> Choose a number from {01 to 25}; numbers from (1 to 9) must be entered as 01,07 etc.***

***-> Don't choose the same number twice.***

***-> The choosen number will be crossed from your as well as BOT's boxes.***

***-> A line means all crossed numbers in a vertical, horizontal or diagonal way in the BOX.***

***-> Completing a line of crossed numbers gives you a letter from "BINGO".***

***-> Reaching the "BINGO" First will win you the Game. Else BOT wins.***

**$ WARNING:- Don't try to enter invalid numbers.**
